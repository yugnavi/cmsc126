ABOUT CSM

HISTORY

The College of Science and Mathematics (CSM) was created on July 1, 1997 by virtue of the Board of Regent’s approval of a proposal by Dean Rogelio V. Cuyno. The faculty and staff of the School of Agri-Systems and Environment (SASE) as well as the Natural Science and Mathematics Cluster of the College of Arts and Sciences (CAS) were re-assigned to the new College. On the other hand, the Humanities and Social Sciences Clusters of the old College of Arts and Sciences became the new College of Humanities and Social Sciences. The SASE and CAS were effectively dissolved with the formation of the new units. The Board’s approval provided that a Director who will function as the Dean of the College would head CSM.

ACADEMIC DEPARTMENTS

The College of Science and Mathematics (CSM) studies the endemic flora and pioneering scientific development in the fields of biology, food science, applied mathematics, and computer science.

Department of Biological Sciences and Environmental Studies

The Department of Biological Sciences and Environmental Studies offers one undergraduate degree program which is the Bachelor of Science in Biology (BSBio).

Department of Food Science and Chemistry


The Department of Food Science and Chemistry offers one undergraduate degree program and one graduate degree program. Its degree programs are the Bachelor of Science in Food Technology (BSFT) and Master of Science in Food Science.

Department of Mathematics, Physics and Computer Science

The Department of Mathematics, Physics and Computer Science is the largest department of the College of Science and Mathematics, UP Mindanao. It offers two of the four undergraduate degree programs in the College. Around fifty percent (50%) of the College's faculty members and students belongs to the department. Its degree programs are the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) and the Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics (BSAM).
